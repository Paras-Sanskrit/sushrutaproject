                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.3">[3] atha khalu bhagavantam amaravaram ṛṣigaṇaparivṛttam

                        ūcuḥ |</l>


                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.4">[4] bhagavañ śārīramānasāgantubhir vyādhibhir
                        vividhavedanābhighātopadrutān sanāthān anāthavad viceṣṭamānān vikrośataś ca
                        mānavān abhisamīkṣya manasi naḥ pīḍābhavat |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.4b">[4] teṣāṃ sukhaiṣiṇāṃ rogopaśamārtham ātmanaś ca
                        prāṇayātrārtham āyurvedaṃ icchāma upadiśyamānam | atrāyattam aihikam
                        āmuṣmikañ ca śreyas tad bhagavantam upasannāḥ smaḥ śiṣyatveneti |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.5">[5] tān uvāca bhagavān svāgatam vaḥ sarva evāmīmāṃsyā
                        adhyāpyāś ca bhavanto vatsāḥ |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.6">[6] iha khalv āyurvedo nāma <hi>yad</hi> upāṅgam
                        atharvavedasyoktam anutpādyaiva ca prajāḥ ślokaśatasahasram adhyāyasahasrañ
                        ca kṛtavān svayambhūr alpāyuṣkālpamedhastvañ cālokya narāṇām bhūyo 'ṣṭadhā
                        praṇītavān | </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.7">[7] tad yathā  śalyaṃ śālākyaṃ kāyacikitsā bhūtavidyākaumārabhṛtyam
                        agadatantraṃ rasāyanatantraṃ vājīkaraṇatantram iti |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.1">[8] athāsya pratyekāṅgalakṣaṇasamāsaḥ |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.1a">[9] tatra śalyan nāma
                        vividhatṛṇakāṣṭhapāṣāṇapāṃsulohaloṣṭāsthibālanakhapūyāsrāvaduṣṭavraṇāntargarbhaśalyoddharaṇārthaṃ
                        yantraśastrakṣārāgnipraṇidhānaviniścayārthañ ca ṣaṣṭyābhidhānair iti | </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.2">[10] śālākyatantran nāmordhvajatrugatānāṃ vikārāṇāṃ
                        śravaṇanayanavadanaghrāṇādisaṃśritānāṃ vikārāṇām upaśamakaraṇārtham |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.3">[11] kāyacikītsā nāma sarvaśarīrāvasthitānāṃ vyādhīnām
                        upaśamakaraṇārthaṃ jvaraśophagulmaraktapittonmādāpasmārapramehātisārādīnāñ
                        ca |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.4">[12] bhūtavidyā nāma
                        devagandharvayakṣarākṣasapitṛpiśācavināyakanāgagrahopasṛṣṭacetasāṃ
                        śāntikarmabaliharaṇādigrahopaśamanārthaṃ |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.5">[13] kaumārabhṛtyan nāma
                        kumārabharaṇadhātrīkṣīradoṣasaṃśodhanārthaṃ duṣṭastanyagrahasamutthitānāñ ca
                        vyādhīnām upaśamakaraṇārtham |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.6">[8.6] agadatantran nāma sarpakīṭadaṣṭaviṣavyañjanārthaṃ
                        vividhaviṣavegopaśamanārthañ ca |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.7">[15] rasāyanatantran nāma vayaḥsthāpanam āyurmedhākaraṇaṃ
                        vyādhyupaśamakaraṇārthañ ca|</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.8.8">[16] vājīkaraṇatantran nāma
                        svalpaduṣṭakṣīṇaviśuṣkaretasāṃ śukrāpyāyanaprasādopacayajanananimittaṃ
                        praharṣajananārthañ ca |</l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.1.1.9">[17] evam ayam āyurvedo 'ṣṭāṅga upadiśyate atra kasmai kiṃ
