                    <head>prathamo'dhyāyaḥ|</head>
                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.1"><label>śā.1.1</label> athātaḥ sarvabhūtacintāśārīraṁ
                        vyākhyāsyāmaḥ|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.2"><label>śā.1.2</label> yathovāca bhagavān dhanvantariḥ|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.3"><label>śā.1.3</label> sarvabhūtānāṁ kāraṇamakāraṇaṁ
                        sattvarajastamolakṣaṇamaṣṭarūpamakhilasya jagataḥ saṁbhavaheturavyaktaṁ
                        nāma| tadekaṁ <ref rend="superscript footnote marker"
                            >1</ref>bahūnāṁ<note type="footnote">'anekeṣāṁ'iti pā+</note>
                        kṣetrajñānāmadhiṣṭhānaṁ samudra ivaudakānāṁ bhāvānām|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.4"><label>śā.1.4</label> tasmādavyaktānmahānutpadyate talliṅga
                        eva; talliṅgāc ca mahatastallakṣaṇa evāhaṅkāra utpadyate, sa trividho
                        vaikārikastaijaso bhūtādiriti; tatra
                        vaikārikādahaṅkārāttaijasasahāyāttallakṣaṇānyenaikādaśendriyāṇyutpadyante,
                        tadyathā---śrotratvakcakṣurjihvāghrāṇavāgghastopasthapāyupādamanāṁsīti,
                        tatra pūrvāṇi pañca buddhīndriyāṇi, itarāṇi pañca karmendriyāṇi,
                        ubhayātmakaṁ manaḥ; bhūtāderapi taijasasahāyāttallakṣaṇānyeva
                        pañcatanmātrāṇyutpadyante, tadyathā---śabdatanmātraṁ, sparśatanmātraṁ,
                        rūpatanmātraṁ, rasatanmātraṁ, gandhatanmātramiti; teṣāṁ viśeṣāṁ viśeṣāḥ
                        śabdasparśarūparasagandhāḥ; tebhyo bhūtāni vyomānilānalajalorvyaḥ; evameṣā
                        tattvacaturviśatirvyākhyātā|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.5"><label>śā.1.5</label> tatra, buddhīndriyāṇāṁ śabdādayo
                        viṣayāḥ; karmendriyāṇāṁ yathāsaṅkhyaṁ vacanādānānandavisargaviharaṇāni|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.6"><label>śā.1.6</label> avyaktaṁ mahānahaṅkāraḥ
                        pañcatanmātrāṇi cetyaṣṭau prakṛtayaḥ; śeṣāḥ ṣoḍaśa vikārāḥ|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.7"><label>śā.1.7</label> svaḥ svaścaiṣāṁ viṣayo'dhibhūtaṁ;
                        svayamadhyātmaṁ; <ref rend="superscript footnote marker"
                            >1</ref>adhidaivataṁ<note type="footnote">'adhidaivatamatha'iti
                            pā+</note> tu---budherbrahyā, ahaṅkārasyeśvaraḥ, manasaścandramāḥ, diśaḥ
                        śrotrasya, tvaco vāyuḥ, sūryaścakṣuṣaḥ, rasanasyāpaḥ, pṛthivī ghrāṇasya,
                            <ref rend="superscript footnote marker">1</ref>vāco'gniḥ<note
                            type="footnote">'vacaso'gniḥ'iti pā+</note>, hastayorindraḥ,
                        pādayorviṣṇuḥ, pāyormitraḥ, prajāpatirupasthasyeti|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.8"><label>śā.1.8</label> tatra sarva evācetana eṣa vargaḥ,
                        puruṣaḥ pañcaviṁśatitamaḥ kāryakāraṇasaṁyuktaścetayitā bhavati|
                        satyapyacaitanye pradhānasya puruṣakaivalyārthaṁ pravṛttimupadiśanti
                        kṣīrādīṁścātra hetūnudāharanti|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.9"><label>śā.1.9</label> ata ūrdhvaṁ prakṛtipuruṣayoḥ
                        sādharmyavaidharmye vyākhyāsyāmaḥ| tadyathā---ubhāvapyanādī,
                        ubhāvapyanantau, ubhāvapyaliṅgau, ubhāvapi nityau, <ref rend="superscript
                            footnote marker">1</ref>ubhāvapyanaparau<note type="footnote"
                            >'ubhāvapyaparau'iti pā+</note>, ubhau ca sarvagatāviti; ekā tu
                        prakṛtiracetanā triguṇā bījadharmiṇī prasavadharmiṇyamadhyasthadharmiṇī
                        ceti, bahavas tu puruṣāścetanāvanto'guṇā abījadharmāṇo'prasavadharmāṇo
                        madhyasthadharmāṇaśceti|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.10"><label>śā.1.10</label> tatra kāraṇānurūpaṁ kāryamiti kṛtvā
                        sarva evaite viśeṣāḥ sattvarajastamomayā bhavanti;
                        tadañjanatvāttanmayatvāc ca tadguṇā eva puruṣā bhavantītyeke bhāṣante|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.11"><label>śā.1.11</label> vaidyake tu--- svabhāvamīśvaraṁ
                        kālaṁ yaddacchāṁ niyatiṁ tathā|| pariṇāmaṁ ca manyante prakṛtiṁ
                        pṛthudarśinaḥ|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.12"><label>śā.1.12</label> tanmayānyeva bhūtāni tadguṇānyeva
                        cādiśet|| taiś ca tallakṣaṇaḥ kṛtsno bhūtagrāmo vyajanyata|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.13"><label>śā.1.13</label> tasyopayogo'bhihitaścikitsāṁ prati
                        sarvadā|| bhūtebhyo hi paraṁ yasmānnāsti cintā cikitsite|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.14"><label>śā.1.14</label>
                        yato'bhihitaṁ---tatsambhavadravyasamūho bhūtādiruktaḥ; bhautikāni
                        cendriyāṇyāyurvede varṇyante, tathendriyārthaḥ|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.15"><label>śā.1.15</label> bhavati cātra---
                        indriyeṇendiyārthaṁ tu svaṁ svaṁ gṛhṇāti mānavaḥ|| niyataṁ
                        tulyayonitvānnānyenānyamiti sthitiḥ|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.16"><label>śā.1.16</label> na cāyurvedaśāstreṣūpadiśyante
                        sarvagatāḥ kṣetrajñā nityāśca; asarvagateṣu ca kṣetrajñeṣu
                        nityapuruṣakhyāpakān hetūnudāharanti; <ref rend="superscript footnote
                            marker">1</ref>āyurvedaśāstrasiddhānteṣvasarvagatāḥ kṣetrajñā
                        nityāśca, tiryagyonimānuṣadeveṣu saṁcaranti dharmādharmanimittaṁ; ta
                        ete'numānagrāhyāḥ paramasūkṣmāścetanāvantaḥ śāśvatā lohitaretasoḥ
                        sannipāteṣvabhivyajyante, yato'bhihitaṁ---pañcamahābhūtaśarīrisamavāyaḥ
                        puruṣa iti; sa eṣa(eva?) karmapuruṣaścikitsādhikṛtaḥ|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.17"><label>śā.1.17</label> tasya sukhaduḥkhe icchādveṣau
                        prayatnaḥ prāṇāpānāvunmeṣanimeṣau buddhirmanaḥ saṅkalpo vicāraṇā
                        smṛtirvijñānamadhyavasāyo viṣayopalabdhiś ca guṇāḥ|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.18"><label>śā.1.18</label> sāttvikāstu---ānṛśaṁsyaṁ
                        saṁvibhāgarucitā titikṣā satyaṁ dharma āstikyaṁ jñānaṁ buddhirmedhā
                        smṛtirdhṛtiranabhiṣaṅgaśca;
                        rājasāstu---duḥkhabahulatā'ṭanaśīlatā'dhṛtirahaṅkāra ānṛtikatvamakāruṇyaṁ
                        dambho māno harṣaḥ krodhaśca; tāmasāstu---viṣāditvaṁ nāstikyamadharmaśīlatā
                        buddhernidho'jñānaṁ durmedhastvamakarmaśīlatā nidrālutvaṁ ceti|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.19"><label>śā.1.19</label> āntarikṣāstu---śabdaḥ śabdendriyaṁ
                        sarvacchidrasamūho viviktatā ca; vāyavyāstu---sparśaḥ sparśondriyaṁ
                        sarvaceṣṭāsamūhaḥ sarvaśarīraspandanaṁ laghutā ca; taijasāstu---rūpaṁ
                        rūpendriyaṁ varṇaḥ santāpo bhrājiṣṇutā paktiramarṣastaikṣṇyaṁ śauryaṁ ca;
                        āpyāstu---raso rasanendriyaṁ sarvadravasamūho gurutā śaityaṁ sneho retaśca;
                        pārthivāstu---gandho gandhendriyaṁ sarvamūrtasamūho gurutā ceti|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.20"><label>śā.1.20</label> tatra sattvabahulamākāśaṁ,
                        rajobahulo vāyuḥ, sattvarajobahulo'gniḥ, sattvatamobahulā āpaḥ, tamobahulā
                        pṛthivīti|| </l>

                    <l xml:id="SS.3.1.21"><label>śā.1.21</label> ślokau cātra bhavataḥ|</l>

                    <lg>

                        <l xml:id="SS.3.1.21cd">sve sve dravye tu sarveṣāṁ vyaktaṁ
                            lakṣaṇamiṣyate||</l>

                    </lg>
